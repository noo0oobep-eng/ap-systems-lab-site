How AP Link Audit looks

What AP Link Audit does

AP Link Audit is a lightweight internal linking audit plugin for WordPress. It scans your published content and highlights:

  • Orphan content – posts and pages with no inbound internal links.
  • Underlinked content – items with only 1–2 inbound internal links.
  • A simple priority list – so you know what to fix first.

It runs as a manual audit. You’re in control of when you scan, what you export, and how you action the results.
