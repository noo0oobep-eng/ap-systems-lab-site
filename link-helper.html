FAQ

Does the free version stay useful on its own?

Yes. The free version is designed to stand on its own as a simple, editor-based suggestion tool. Pro is an optional upgrade for extra speed and control.

Does this plugin add anything to the front end?

No. It’s an editor helper only. It doesn’t inject widgets or banners on the public site.

How do I get Pro after I pay?

After checkout via Stripe, you’ll receive a download link in your receipt. Download the ZIP, upload it to WordPress as a plugin, and activate it alongside the free version.

Will you add more features?

Yes, but carefully. The goal is to keep this focused and understandable. Future updates will stay aligned with that — making internal linking easier without turning it into another complex system to manage.
