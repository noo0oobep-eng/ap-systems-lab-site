About AP Systems Lab

AP Systems Lab is a small studio focused on practical WordPress tools. The goal is simple: help site owners make better decisions with less noise. That means choosing problems worth solving, resisting feature sprawl, and building plugins that we’re happy to support for years.

We start with real workflows: how people actually write, edit, and maintain their sites. From there we design tools that stay close to WordPress itself — minimal setup, clean UI, predictable behaviour. Free versions are complete and useful on their own; Pro versions add depth, not basic access gates.

AP Link Audit is the first of a small family of plugins aimed at content structure: seeing where internal links are missing, deciding what to fix first, and building a healthier site over time without a huge SEO stack.

FAQ

Will the free version stay free?

Yes. AP Link Audit will remain a free, fully-functional internal linking audit plugin.

Does the plugin change my theme or front end?

No. It only scans content and shows results in a WordPress admin screen.

How do I get Pro after purchase?

After checkout, Stripe will email you a receipt with a download link and short setup guide. Install it like any other plugin and activate it alongside AP Link Audit.

Can I use it on multiple sites?

Yes. The code is GPL-friendly — you can use it on as many sites as you manage. We just ask that you keep the ZIP private and point others to the official purchase link.
